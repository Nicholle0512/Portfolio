03 / Maker

About the work

Hi, I'm Nicholle
Np
N. Perkins ● Fig. 01

My goal is to create experiences that are both functional and emotionally resonant. Not just websites. Not just games. Something that stays with you.

I'm a frontend developer and creative technologist based in Virginia. I spend most of my time in the quiet overlap between code and feeling — the place where a loading animation becomes a breath, or a hover state becomes a small piece of joy. I care about how things work, but I care a little more about how they feel.

When I'm not building, I'm sketching small games, reading about type, or taking things apart to see what makes them tick. Currently open to thoughtful teams, unusual briefs, and anything that involves making the web a little less boring.
